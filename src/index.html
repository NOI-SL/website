What is International Olympiad in Informatics ?

It is an algorithmic programming competition held annually for secondary school students. At IOI, contestants have to write programs in C++, Pascal or Java to solve a set of problems within a specified time limit.

Every year, the member countries of IOI send 4 contestants to compete. Sri Lanka has taken part in this competition since 1992. IOI 2018 was held in Japan with the participation of 335 participants from 87 countries around the world.

IOI 2019 is to be held next year in Azerbaijan (www.ioi2019.az).

What is Sri Lankan Olympiad in Informatics ?

Sri Lankan Olympiad in Informatics is a programming contest for secondary school students, organized by University of Colombo School of Computing to improve the knowledge and skill of computing in young students.

Additionally, students to represent Sri Lanka at the International Olympiad in Informatics program are selected through this competition.

The national competition comprises of a few stages:

  • Online IQ test
  • C++ Programming training camp
  • National training pool selection test
  • Algorithms training camp
  • National Olympiad in Informatics competition
  • IOI national team selection test
  • Awarding ceremony
